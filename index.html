Welcome to WilHack.

Bringing together developers, Professors, and Tech Sponsors for our first 14-hour hackathon competition. Join us for one epic event and see if you have what it takes to build the best app in 14 hours.

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an event in which computer programmers, graphical artists and project managers collaborate on software projects. A hackathon can last anywhere from three to forty-eight hours in length and brings together some of the best upcoming minds in the tech industry. Network with some of the best companies in the tech industry and build something!

Why participate in a hackathon?

Hackathons provide students with opportunities to learn new software technologies, hardware and gain real-world programming experience outside of the classroom, and make connections with professionals in the field. Build something cool and present your hack! Learn, have fun, and win prizes!

How does WilHack fit in?

Formal computer science classes provide a basis for programming, but many students don't know where to start in order to turn their innovative ideas into realities. We show students the ropes when it comes to source control, app development, and practical applications of coding knowledge. WilHack fosters innovation by providing a mentor process that bridges the gap between the classroom and professional computer science community.

Who can come?

We welcome undergraduate, graduate students, software developers, and professors from all universities, you must have a valid Identification.

How to I sign up?

Signups are open unti l November 1st. Kindly follow the eventbrite link below to register. Registration is free.

What if I have dietary restrictions?

We've got you covered! We will make sure to set aside meals that satisfies your allergy or dietary condition. All we ask is that you let us know when you confirm your invitation.

Do I need a ton of experience?

Not at all! We welcome students of all skill levels. We have an incredible staff of mentors here to help out if you're just getting started, or even if you've been coding since you could walk.

How can I get to WilHack?

Unfortunately, we will not cover transportation expense for this event.

What should I bring?

Laptops, cellphones, and chargers tend to be helpful at events such as WilHack. We recommend non-Wiley students also pack a change of clothes, and toiletries. Just nothing dangerous please. Of course, don't forget to bring your friends and teammates!

What big can my team be?

Teams are composed of 2-4 people, involving any combination of school, age and gender. Though participants may work alone, we highly encourage you to join a group and make new friends!

What will the weekend look like?

Our opening ceremony kicks off early Friday evening, and general fun ensues until Sunday afternoon, when we bid adieu and all get some shuteye. There will be plenty of awesomeness in between.
